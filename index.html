Business Intelligence in the Cloud

The goal of modern business intelligence deployments is to support better business decision-making. Often BI applications use data gathered from a data warehouse or a data mart. However, cloud computing provides an alternative way.

Cloud-based or software-as-a-service (SaaS) solutions can be easily fit to companies of all shapes and sizes. This allows even the smallest businesses the ability to tailor a BI system to fit their needs and improve their businesses. Moreover, unlike traditional site-licensed softwarema cloud-based BI solution, upfront cost is reduced because all hardware and software systems are located and managed remotely by vendors and service providers.

Trend: More and more business are working on improveing the security of cloud computing platforms.

Mine the Connections among Stocks

It has been widely accepted that Domino Effect of Board Linkage exist in security market. Intuitively thinking, prices of certain stocks tend to go up and down together, either because they are in the same industry or because they are from the same region.In other words, such each stocks logical connected, prediction. This is meaningful for portfolio management idea of avoiding putting eggs in the same basket.

Here we provide an interactive demo to demonstrate how such data mining techniques help improve human's understanding of the stock market. Given the stocks (specified by symbols) in the textbox below, we are able to identify their structure from the price correlations in the recent 300 days, and visualize such structure with a graph.

Say we have 100 stocks, and daily prices for this year. So we get a 100 by 250 dimension matrix, roughly (weekends). Each stock is represented by a vector. The number of component is too large to estimate.Then we use PCA(principal component analysis) Analysis through dimension reduction and extracting principal component. After visualization, each stock is represented by a dot on a coordinate, and these dots falls into several clusters.

You can also modify the symbols by yourself and see our algorithm's observation towards the market. Type in the symbol of any listed security and see what happens.

Human Versus Machine

Here I propose a statistical model to explore the ability of a computer to forecast human being's choice. A Markov model is formulated in which the latent state space contains both pure and mixed strategies, and allows switching between these states.

In the following simplified "Scissors Rock Papaer" game, you will see machine learns from previous repeated gaming. A computer always follows its part of the unique mixed strategy Nash equilibrium profile.

On the other hand, estimated results show significant mixed strategy play and non-stationary dynamics.this model could assess whether individuals strategically use mixed strategies in repeated games.
