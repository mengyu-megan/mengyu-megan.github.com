Stock Clustering utilizing JDBC and Machine Learning

The underlying logic of this project is to collects stock market data from the Internet with Java, stores data in SQL Server, and does analysis and visualization with MATLAB.
Intuitively thinking, prices of certain stocks tend to go up and down together, either because they are in the same industry or because they are from the same region.
In other words, such each stocks logical connected, prediction. This is meaningful for portfolio management, avoid put eggs in the same basket.
Say we have 100 stocks, and daily prices for this year. So we get a 100 by 250 dimension matrix, roughly (weekends). Each stock is represented by a vector. The number of component is too large to estimate.Then we use PCA(principal component analysis) Analysis,ML techniques. Dimension reduction, extract principal component. . After visualization, each stock is represented by a dot on a coordinate, and these dots falls into several clusters.

Human Versus Machine

Here I propose a statistical model to explore the ability of a computer to forecast human being's action choice.to assess whether individuals strategically use mixed strategies in repeated games. A Markov model is formulated in which the latent state space contains both pure and mixed strategies, and allows switching between these states. You will see machine learns from previous repeated gaming that a computer always follows its part of the unique mixed strategy Nash equilibrium profile.Estimated results show significant mixed strategy play and non-stationary dynamics.
